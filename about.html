Who We Are

InterviewEdge was founded in Chennai, Tamil Nadu by a team passionate about helping India's youth find employment opportunities. We understand the challenge of finding reliable, up-to-date interview information in one place.

Our team manually curates and verifies interview listings from official company websites, career portals, and trusted sources before publishing them on our platform. We prioritise accuracy and timeliness.

We currently cover freshers drives, experienced professional openings, campus recruitments, and walk-in interviews across all major Indian cities and remote opportunities.
